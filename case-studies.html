Kitchen Sink & Tap Transformation

📍 Derbyshire · November 2023

The customer wanted to modernise their kitchen by replacing a worn stainless steel sink with a premium ceramic alternative. The old tap was also swapped for a stylish flexible-head chrome mixer.

The job involved removing the existing sink unit, adapting the waste pipework to suit the new ceramic basin's configuration, and fitting the new tap with updated supply connections — all completed in a single visit.

✦ Boiler Installation

Back Boiler to Combi Conversion

📍 Derbyshire

This property had an old, inefficient back boiler taking up valuable kitchen space with bulky flue pipework running up the wall. The customer wanted a modern, compact combi to free up space and cut energy bills.

We stripped out the old system, re-routed pipework in neat copper runs, and installed a new Main combi boiler — clean, compact, and significantly more efficient.
