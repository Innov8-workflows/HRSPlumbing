Our Story
Built on
Trust &

HRS Plumbing & Heating Ltd was founded with a simple goal: to provide homeowners across Derbyshire with a plumbing and heating service they can genuinely rely on. No hidden costs, no corners cut, no fuss.

We specialise in the installation, maintenance, and servicing of all general plumbing and natural gas heating systems. Whether it's a boiler replacement, a leaking pipe, or a full bathroom fit-out, we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every job.

Being a local, independent business means we have real accountability to our customers and community. Every job we complete is a testament to our reputation — and we take that seriously.
